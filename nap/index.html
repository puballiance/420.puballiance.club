No Attack Policy

Rules

Rule #1

All alliance members under top 20 alliances will not attack or scout alliances in these 20 alliances as well as their sub alliance and their farm alliances.

Rule #2

All 20 alliances will have only 1 sub alliance and 4 farm alliances for each NAP 20 alliances and all of them should be added to your alliance wall. Farm alliance can only have a maximum of 9 real players as R5 and R4. The rest of the members should have "farm" in their name for avoiding misuse of farm alliance as sub alliance.

Rule #3

Under any situation when an alliance member in NAP 20 attacks a NAP 20 alliance, R5 of alliance which got attacked can choose to forgive the attacker after talking to him first or attack the member who attacked in the same magnitude of the attack received. (Eg: Zeroed if the attacker zeroed the NAP member.)

Rule #4

For any type of violation of NAP by an alliance, their whole hive will not be attacked unless their R5 decides to break NAP intentionally. This will result in expulsion of this alliance from NAP and State council until the rest of the state council members choose to let them come back.

Rule #5

Any member who break NAP under any situation will should be kicked out of his alliance if R5 of the NAP alliance who got attacked request R5 of attacker's alliance.

Rule #6

Do not attack players 10 levels lower than you without them attacking first. Hence it creates a space for small players to grow.

Rule #7

NAP 20 will be void on any NAP 20 member entering Launch Center area.

Rule #8

State vs State Kill event days will be ALL NAP. No tag bases are free to attack. On other Kill Events, NAP 20 will have no changes.

Rule #9

NAP 20 alliances who are not following NAP 20 rules will be having their NAP suspended until they correct it. Any attacks on their alliance or sub alliance or farm alliance under NAP suspension will not be considered as violation of the NAP 20.

These conditions are subjected to change if majority of state council members vote for change.

F.A.Q.

When should I port?

You can port when you have 2000 diamonds or 1 advance teleport and your base name is listed in the move list.

Where is the move list?

Move list is published in "All Mail" inside the game.

How do I know if I ported to the right location?

Touch on your base after porting and see if the coordinated under the base and coordinates given to you as same. If they are different, then you made a wrong port. But you dont have to port again even if you made a wrong port. The hole made in new hive will be filled in next move list.
